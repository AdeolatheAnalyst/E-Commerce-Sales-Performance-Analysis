# E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Analysis

## Project Overview
Analyzing sales data to uncover trends, insights and business recommendations to smart data-driven decisions for sustainable growth.

## Business Questions
-Which product category generate the most revenue?

-Which customer segment contributes the highest revenue?

-How do customer ratings vary across product categories, and which category performs best overall?

-What time period performs best?


## Tools Used
Excel, Power BI

## Dataset
Source:Kaggle

## Methodology
1. **Data Verification in Excel**  
   - Checked for duplicate rows to avoid double-counting
  
   - Checked for missing values and ensured correct formats

2. **Visualization & Analysis in Power BI**  
   - Created charts to compare average ratings by product category
      
   - Aggregated revenue by customer segment to identify top contributors
      
   - Analyzed discount impact on purchases across product categories

## Insights
-This company thrives on two forces;
A loyal customer base and a powerful Electronics category, 

-While Sports quietly waits as the next big growth opportunity, fueled by top ratings and the highest demand.

## Recommendations
-Pull back on unnecessary Electronics discounts to protect profit margins.

-Study peak sales months and replicate what worked to smooth out revenue dips.

-Lightly discount and promote Sports products to unlock their full revenue potential.

-Double down on retention strategies, because loyal customers already carry the business.
